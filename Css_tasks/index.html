Employee Payroll System

Work Flow 

 Employee: The base class containing basic attributes such as ID, name, department, phone, and leave balance. It is extended by FullTimeEmployee, PartTimeEmployee.
 FullTimeEmployee: Represents employees on a full-time basis and includes an additional attribute for salary.
PartTimeEmployee: Represents employees on a part-time basis and includes attributes for hourly rate and hours worked.
EmployeePayroleSystem: Manages a list of employees and provides methods to perform operations such as adding/removing employees, calculating total payroll, displaying employee information, managing leave, and generating payslips.
